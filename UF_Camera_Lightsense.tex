\newline
\textbf{Mount the camera and lightsense board  --- }
\begin{itemize}
\item Connect lightsense board to outside of 3D printed mount and camera to inside facing out through hole in lightsense board, make sure camera is facing down toward base of the mount (the USB8MP02G label should be upsidedown near the base).
\item Thread the screws through lightsense board, then mount, then camera (putting black washers at both ends) with nuts on camera side.
\end{itemize}
}
