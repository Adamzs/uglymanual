			\item Uncouple the hexnut from the cable gland, the washer should remain on the gland.
			\item Install the cable gland in the hole opposite to the AC pigtail from the outside of the plastic box. The rubber washer should
			be on the outside of the box.
			\item Install the hexnut on the gland from inside the box, but keep loose.
			\item Push the red, black and green wire end of the DC Power Assembly ~\textit{AOTPN-06} through the cable gland from inside the box.
			\item Pull the cable through until the cable gland is within heat-shrunk portion of the DC Power Assembly.
			\item Tighten the gland compressor nut and secure the cable in the gland.
		\end{itemize}
	}
